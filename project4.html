Brief

EPCO Corp. is embarking an exciting collaboration with its partner to introduce a new collaboration brand - Sheerglaze. It is a US-based manufacturer and supplier to the glass and glazing industries. Our brand provides a complete range of innovative architectural hardware for frameless shower doors, all-glass entrances, aluminum storefronts, and entrances.

In this project, I worked as the sole designer in the team to create the branding from scratch to final. We aimed to establish a strong and clear brand identity while developing the packageing design that is both cost-effective and user-friendly.
